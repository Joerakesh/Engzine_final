About

Engzine - "Sharing voices, valuing efforts"

ENGZINE, a unique fusion of “English” and “Magazine,” stands as a vibrant testament to the voices, creativity and spirit of Gen Z. Its bold “GZ” isn’t just a stylistic choice but a declaration of its focus: a platform for the new generation to explore, express, and inspire. Published biannually as an e magazine by the Department of English (Shift II), St. Joseph’s College (Autonomous), Trichy – 02. ENGZINE was created with a clear purpose—to nurture and showcase the literary and artistic talents blossoming within the student community.
