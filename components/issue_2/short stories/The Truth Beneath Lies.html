The Truth Beneath Lies

Aishu grew up in a seemingly perfect family, admired by the town for their flawless reputation. However, behind closed doors, she knew things weren’t as they seemed. Her parents’ marriage was strained, marked by late-night arguments and cold silences.

One day, Aishu found a box of old letters from her mother to a man named Chidhambaram, revealing a past love and a painful affair that her mother had hidden to protect the family. When Aishu confronted her mother, she learned the truth: her mother had sacrificed her happiness for the sake of her marriage.

Though the truth shattered the image of perfection Aishu had once believed in, it freed her from the lies. Aishu realized that honesty, no matter how painful, was the only way to truly move forward…
