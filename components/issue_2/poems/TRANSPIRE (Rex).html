TRANSPIRE

Through Mist and Shadow, Dawn Takes Flight,

A Whispered Dream in Golden Light.

Time Unfolds Like Petals Wide,

Revealing Truths We Cannot Hide.


The Winds May Change, The Stars May Roam,

Yet Fate Will Carve What’s Yet Unknown.

From Depths Untold, We Rise, Aspire,

To Bloom, To Soar—To Transpire.

by Allwin Rex K
