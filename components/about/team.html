About Our Student Team

The heartbeat of ENGZINE, our dynamic e-magazine, lies in its exceptional team of five dedicated students and a committed staff in-charge. Together, they have brought this visionary project to life with relentless effort and creativity. From conceptualization to execution, the ENGZINE team has orchestrated every aspect of this literary journey. Their responsibilities have been both diverse and demanding

The team initiated the process by organizing engaging orientation sessions to spark interest and auditions to select the best contributors for the magazine. Regular updates and reminders ensured contributors remained informed and motivated throughout the process. Whether coordinating with contributors on submissions or troubleshooting technical issues, the team efficiently handled it all with grace and professionalism. Creativity met precision as the students meticulously designed the layout, ensuring every page reflected the essence of literary and visual artistry. The team also ventured into the digital frontier by designing a user-friendly and visually appealing webpage for ENGZINE, enhancing its reach and accessibility.

Under the guidance of the staff in-charge, this student-led initiative stands as a testament to their passion and perseverance. Tirelessly working behind the scenes, they have turned challenges into milestones, ensuring that ENGZINE is not just a magazine but a community-driven celebration of creativity and talent.
