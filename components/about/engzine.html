About Our Magazine

"Sharing voices and valuing efforts" is the heartbeat of ENGZINE, a biannual department literary magazine that celebrates the diverse essence of campus life in its first issue. Every page reflects the affection, creativity, and insight of individuals who have worked together to make this edition an unforgettable experience. ENGZINE is more than just a collection of works.

This edition explores the vibrant pulse of student life, capturing inspiring, thoughtful, and joyful moments. The poetry section evokes feelings of joy, nostalgia, and meditation, while the short stories transport readers to imaginative worlds with distinct viewpoints. In addition, we feature photographs, drawings, quizzes, and podcast interviews with students and teachers discussing literature, culture, and campus life, creating a multifaceted experience for ENGZINE.

Every voice, idea, and story about campus life contributes to the collective narrative of our community, making it a dynamic ecosystem. ENGZINE provides a platform where creativity and intellectual curiosity converge, showcasing the unique experiences and perspectives of staff, students, alumni, and volunteers. We hope this edition inspires imagination and creativity, as it reflects the lives and viewpoints of our instructors and students. This magazine would not be possible without the contributions of our writers, artists, and creators, as well as the support of our professors. We express our deepest gratitude to St. Joseph's College for fostering an environment that encourages innovation and intellectual development.
