About Our Magazine

A unique fusion of “English” and “Magazine,” stands as a vibrant testament to the voices, creativity and spirit of Gen Z. Its bold “GZ” isn’t just a stylistic choice but a declaration of its focus: a platform for the new generation to explore, express, and inspire. Published biannually as an e magazine by the Department of English (Shift II), St. Joseph’s College (Autonomous), Trichy – 02. ENGZINE was created with a clear purpose—to nurture and showcase the literary and artistic talents blossoming within the student community.

Guided by the motto “Sharing Voices, Valuing Efforts,” ENGZINE invites students, Alumni, Faculty Members to bring their stories and visions to life through a variety of expressive forms. Within its digital pages, readers will find thought provoking essays, evocative poetry, imaginative short stories, striking paintings, and powerful photography etc. Through videography, podcasts, and interviews, ENGZINE also captures the pulse of today’s generation in multimedia format, offering a dynamic experience for creators and readers alike.

This magazine isn’t just a collection of works; it’s a community. A space where mainly students can develop their craft as editors, proofreaders, and creators, fostering a network of emerging writers and artists bound by a passion for storytelling and creativity. ENGZINE invites readers to join in this journey, embracing fresh perspectives, sparking curiosity, and celebrating the unique, diverse voices of the next generation of storytellers.
