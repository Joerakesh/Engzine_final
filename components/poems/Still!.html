Still!

Campus ignites

My dead coal torches

Thousand young minds

Scattered on earth nook and corner…

Still!


Teaching slips into

Multifaceted forms

Stretches boundless wings

Fluttering against the ranks…

Still!


Zen touch a painless persuasion

Remains painful when

Gen Z sings

Muttering against the morals…

Still!


Academic strain

Slowly feigns my brain

Only slogan

Performance based…

Still!


Teaching freezes

Technique breeches

Reminiscence whispers

You are a teacher…

Still!


Dream triggers

My fall forms

Unstoppable ripples

Touching my reason…

Still!


With all shortfalls

Never fails uttering

My campus

Continuous love…

Still!


Everything

Ending In EMI

Endless Mission in creating Intellectual

No end…

Still!

by Dr. J. Charles Arockiasamy
