Whispers of Timeless Youth

In the cradle of dreams, where aspirations soar,

My college campus, a radiant core.

Echoes of mirth, forever begun.

Beneath the venerable oaks, tales softly spun,

Morning dew on verdant blades,

Dawn's hues in delicate cascades.

Classrooms brimming with fervent minds,

A mosaic of thoughts, intricately aligned.

Hallways alive with untold lore,

Friendships blooming, treasures galore.

Library's silence, a hallowed retreat,

Pages turning, hearts in quiet beat.

Cafeteria's hum, a chorus of delight,

Moments of joy, in memory's sight.

Evening strolls by the serene lake,

Reflections of dreams, ripples we make.

Under the moon's tender embrace,

Nights of wonder, a celestial grace.

Stars above, our silent guides,

In this sanctuary, where our spirit abides.

Memories carved by time's gentle hand,

A haven, a promised land.

Whenever I reminisce, my heart takes flight,

Breathing life anew, in the soft twilight.

These timeless days, a cherished song,

In the garden of my soul, they belong.

My college campus, a masterpiece of art,

Forever alive, within my heart

by Subhasini
