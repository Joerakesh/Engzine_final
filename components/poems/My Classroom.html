My Classroom

"A sea of unknown faces, a room so new

Mixed feelings swirled, what to do?

First day of college, a chapter unfolds

Excitement and nerves, young hearts told


Friendships blossomed, amidst doubts and fears

A best friend emerged, through laughter and tears

But amidst the joy, conflicts did arise

Ego and misunderstandings, a toxic surprise


Groups formed, divisions grew

Confusion reigned, what to do?

Judging others, a mistake so grand

A lesson learned, in this classroom land


Yet, amidst the chaos, love did shine

Teachers guided, with hearts so divine

Sharing food, laughter, and glee

A sense of belonging, for you and me


A classroom should be, a haven of peace

Where hearts connect, and love increase

No room for ego, or judgmental eyes

Just love, laughter, and sunny skies"
