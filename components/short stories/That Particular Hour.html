That Particular Hour

Yet again, the students are rushing to the class. There he is, the reason we are running towards the class. I don't know what magic he does to us. I've never seen any PG class rushing for their lectures. The lively class, with so many facts and interesting examples, and the way he delivers everything makes us sit down for 45 minutes. No one will ever get bored in Dr. Imman Harry's class.

He's a psychology professor and has great knowledge and a good perception of life. Anyone could listen to this omniscient person. The moment he sees a person, he will know what the person is going through. Yeah, so many of them are waiting to meet him. But we have the privilege to sit in his class and listen to all his experiences. If anyone sleeps in class, he would know, and he'll bring them back to the class.

He is a very jovial person to talk to. In class, he used to give us a lot of real-life examples from the clients he met (without revealing their identities). The way he handles the subject makes it easier to learn. I could go to the exams without studying—that's how efficiently he teaches. He often travels to other countries, so his class will be limited. That's the reason no one misses his class.

We used to ask him a lot of life-related questions, like what he'll do in his free time after taking our classes. He made a joke there and said he used to write books and read. Yeah, he has written many books. I've two books with me that he wrote: one is “Loving Joyously," and another is in Tamil. We all could spare anything to sit in his class.

Yet again, we are walking towards the class for the last convocation speech from him.

Written by Jebinsha J
