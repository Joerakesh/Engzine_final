Professors guide, mentors navigate,

A sanctuary of learning, love, and light,

St Joseph's College, forever shining through.


In Trichy's heart, a legacy remains,

Producing greatness, in every goal.

Shining bright, like a guiding light.

Where Jesuit ideals, ignite the mind,
