From Start to Forever

In the first minute of our birth

We meant nothing to each other.

At first sight of us,

We were strangers to each other.

In the first meeting of us,

We were classmates for each other.

In the first conversation of us,

We were friends for each other.

In the first problem of us,

We were soulmates for each other.

In the last minute of our deaths,

We will do everything for each other.

From Nothing to Everything

We have come a long way together.

by Merline I
