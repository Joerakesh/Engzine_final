The Poetic Thread of Us

I hold my pen.

Close my eyes for a minute.

Then think about our memories.

I dropped some words.

And it became poetry!

The reason behind my poetry is you.

My heart will always cherish your memories.

The way we enjoyed our college days is still remembered.

The memories that you gave made me a scribbler.

I feel like a blessing to meet people like you.

I still miss all our beautiful moments.

And now I'm seriously longing for those throwback moments.

Though this separation is harder

I want to tell you that

I'm always there for you.

And I will surely be the remedy in your bitter days.

The bond between us is like a sky

No one can ever separate us.

So, like a cloud, we will meet soon.

And I hope that we both cherish our days like olden days.

by Merline
