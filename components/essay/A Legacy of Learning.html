A LEGACY OF LEARNING

Someone has said right that learning is something that takes place throughout our lives. Educational institutions like schools and colleges lay the foundation for the process of learning in one’s life. School memories consist of our childhood friends, lovable teachers, and the fun we had with our friends, and they undoubtedly remain unforgettable memories in one’s life. And after school-based education, we move towards getting ourselves enrolled in colleges. During this phase of life, we tend to be more responsible, and the campus life can be seen as more vibrant. Where every one of us strives to chase our dreams and to make cherishable memories. Campus life ensures to not restrict our learning to books alone and opens the windows to the various aspects of our lives.

Campus life is the most important and priceless aspect of everyone’s life. This is the time when we not only enrich our academic skills but also contribute to making us learn important life skills, which can pave the path for making us an ideal and productive individual for society. This is the place where we get exposure to healthy relationships with our peers. In campus life, we get to learn and understand that one should not only be happy with the success of their own but also be happy for the success of their mate as well. Here I get to learn ways to lead a disciplined life. Campus life, as mentioned earlier, is the most vibrant one. It can be fun-filled experiences with our friends and healthy relationships with our professors, and there are so many aspects that make our life filled with enjoyable moments.

Campus life makes us aware that life consists of both smooth and rough roads and makes us capable of travelling through them effectively to lead the journey of life without any setbacks. We learn here that during times of hardship in our lives, we must remember the happy moments, push ourselves to come out of such hard times, and always keep moving ahead to achieve our goals. Because we know that failure is not the end of our life, rather it is the one that kickstarts our life journey. Certain things in our life may have an end, but memories are the ones that last forever. Campus life is one of those memories that lasts forever in everyone’s life.
