A Heaven of Learning

Introduction:

Identity card, as we all know, is the most important thing on the campus without which college life will not move forward, creating experiences or entry into the field of learning never takes place. It is like an umbilical cord. Just like a child that needs the support of the mother, this ID card connects us with the college and provides all that we need. It has many roles to play in the campus life of a student, who can change and make things better.

A Noose or a Necklace:

There are two main notions about the card. One is that it is an adornment that brings pride and makes one feel comfortable. It gives a student the freedom to explore and enjoy the benefits that this prestigious institution offers. A student with this card will be identified as a person with dignity and honour. On the other hand, an ID card is a big unexpressed burden for both the students and the faculty. Some feel that it is a distraction to their fashion or a heavy laden to carry. The weight of the card is not so much compared to the weight of a phone or a textbook. But it is heavier than we can imagine. The value it has and the purpose it serves add special weight to it. The simple card with a strap can decide the atmosphere around the person. The moment one loses it, especially during the exams, the value increases more than a gold ring or a diamond necklace.

An Instrument of Confidence:

ID card for some becomes an instrument of confidence. People with low self-esteem tend to have something in their hands in order to stay calm or focused or avoid being shy or nervous. But for others, it gives motivation to express themselves confidently because the card that one carries along has a legacy and makes them feel proud about their position as a student in the most esteemed college. The students trust themselves and the place where they stand, just like I do in our college.

Companion:

It is the closest companion which walks, jumps, runs, and does everything with us. It is the greatest companion on the campus, without whose company, the completion of the degree is impossible.

Interdependent and Independent:

The identity it gives also provides freedom to explore and experiment. Freedom with responsibility. It makes all depend on the greatness of the college and oneself individually, and makes the same independent to fly high and shine in the world.

A Valuable Souvenir:

For the people who complete their studies and reach great heights, this small card becomes a small spark that ignites many memories. They would cherish the memories, experiences, and lessons learned. It is not just a simple card; it is the key to opening the door to a new life.

A Mark of Equality:

The ID card is the sign that signifies equality among the students. It gives power to each individual to ask for equality in all aspects of the college. The ID card provides the same treatment to every individual irrespective of their background or status. It also gives a message that each individual is part of this extended family and each one must uphold its honour, be responsible in taking care of it, and sincerely contribute to the greatness of our home.
